Fettuccini Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Butter
  • Ground Black Pepper

Directions

Slowly mix the flour and eggs together, usually in a ratio of 1 egg per 100g of flour. One egg should feed about one person. After the mixture starts to form a dough, knead heavily until the dough becomes smooth and even. Cover with plastic wrap and leave to rest for 30 mins - 1 hour. Roll the pasta with a machine or rolling pin, until they are thin enough to be slightly translucent. (Pasta thickens when it boils!) Cut it into Fettuccini length and width with a pasta machine or with a knife. Boil for only a few minutes! Fresh pasta cooks very quickly. Keep the water you cooked the pasta in, and use that as the base for your sauce. Add generous butter and extra generous grated parmesan cheese. Mix on low heat to emulsify (combine). Toss in pasta and top with black pepper.
