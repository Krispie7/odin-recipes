World's Best Smash Burgers

Ingredients:

  • Burger Buns (or Brioche)
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup
  • Barbeque Sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Butter
  • Any sliced cheese

Seasonings:

  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder

Directions

Sauce

Mix together equal parts ketchup and mayonnaise, with a hint of barbeque sauce

How to prepare Vegetables

  • Wash, dry, and cute lettuce into thin strips, or shred it.
  • Wash and cut tomatoes into quarter inch thick slices.
  • Peel and dice your onion

Lightly butter both sides of your buns, and lightly toast them on a pan to brown the butter. Shape your ground beef into balls. (Meat shrinks when cooked so be generous). Season top half of the meat balls with salt, pepper, onion and garlic powder. Then place the balls of meat onto a hot pan with the seasoned side down. Take something (a small pot works) with parchment paper, and press the meat ball into a flat patty with as much weight as you can use. season the top of the patty with salt, pepper, onion and garlic, powder. Place your choice of cheese on the patty before the last flip. Throw in the onions on the side of the pan, and carmelize them. Once the meat is fully cooked, assemble your burgers:
